Mobile Streaming


Access from multiple devices is a necessity if you are looking to maximise your audience and with online video accounting for 50% of mobile traffic. we understand the importance to reach these platforms. We offer the option to stream to multiple devices on all projects. An employee can watch the video on his mobile while he is not tethered to his workstation. He can watch the video or attend the training session while he is on the road. This way he can utilize his time more efficiently. He can attend an important meeting while he is travelling. The content owners, operators and distributors can broadcast directly to consumers.


Mobile streaming has become increasingly popular with the rise of social media platforms and dedicated mobile apps that enable users to broadcast and share live content with their followers. It is commonly used for a wide range of purposes, including sharing personal moments, live reporting of events, conducting virtual meetings, product demonstrations, gaming, and much more.
